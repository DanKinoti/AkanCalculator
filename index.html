WELCOME TO THE AKAN CALCULATOR


Introduction


The Akan calculator is a handy application that determines your Akan name.

The Calculator does this by using your birthday details such as:

  1. Century your born e.g 1900 or 2000
  2. Year e.g 2002 or 2012 (The last two digits)
  3. The month e.g October
  4. The day e.g 19th
And finally using your gender.


This is one step to getting closer to the Ghanian culture!:)
